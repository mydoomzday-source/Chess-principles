End game

- In chess, the endgame refers to the final phase of the game, following the opening and middlegame, where only a few pieces remain on the board.

It's characterized by a focus on promoting pawns, activating the king, and converting positional advantages into a win.

The endgame differs from earlier stages as the king becomes a more active and important piece, and pawns, especially passed pawns, become crucial for achieving promotion.

Endgame beginner tips:

  1. Master the basic checkmates

  2. Master the basics of technique

  3. Push passed pawns

  4. Activate your king

  5. Find and use the pawn majority

  6. Use the principle of two weaknesess

  7. Be concrete & calculate!

Master these, and you might become one of the grandmasters!
